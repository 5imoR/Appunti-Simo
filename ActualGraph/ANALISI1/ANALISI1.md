### Insiemi
$$
\mathbb{N} \subset \mathbb{Z} \subset \mathbb{Q} \subset \mathbb{R} \subset \mathbb{C}
$$

$\mathbb{N} =$ naturali                $(0,1,2,3,4,5)$
$\mathbb{Z} =$ interi                    $(-2,-1)$
$\mathbb{Q} =$ razionali              $(1/3, -7/3)$
$\mathbb{R} =$ reali                     $(\pi, \sqrt2,\; e)$
$\mathbb{C} =$ complessi            $(3+4i)$
